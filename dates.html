SPADE 2025 - Important Dates

Below are the key dates for SPADE 2025. Please make sure to mark your calendar and adhere to these deadlines.

  • Workshop Paper Submission Deadline: November 1, 2024
  • Notification of Acceptance: December 18, 2024
  • Camera Ready Version and Registration: January 13, 2025
  • Workshop Date: co-located with ICASSP 2025 in Hyderabad, India (April 6 to April 11, 2025)

All deadlines refer to midnight (23:59) in the AoE (Anywhere on Earth) time zone. For the camera-ready version, at least one author of each accepted paper must register for the workshop. Papers that are not presented at the workshop will not be included in the proceedings.

Accepted papers under the Original Research Track will be published in the IEEE Xplore Digital Library.

To submit your paper, please visit the submission website: https://cmt3.research.microsoft.com/SPADE2025/.
