Awards

Best Paper Award

The workshop will feature a best paper award to recognize the most outstanding paper presented at the workshop. The program committee will evaluate all papers based on their originality, technical quality, and relevance to the workshop topics.

The best paper award will be determined using a combination of the review scores and the presentation quality. The award will be presented to the authors of the paper that receives the highest overall score. The best paper award will be announced during the closing session of the workshop and reported in this section.
