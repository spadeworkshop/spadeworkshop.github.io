SPADE 2025 - Call for Papers

Topics of Interest

The topics of interest for SPADE 2025 include, but are not limited to:

  • New techniques for detecting pathologies from speech
  • Speech-based biomarkers for identifying neurodegenerative diseases
  • Early detection of dementia through speech analysis
  • ASR systems tailored to individuals with dysarthria and other speech impairments
  • Methodologies to improve the lives of people with speech-related diseases
  • Customized speech technologies for age-specific and condition-specific populations
  • Non-intrusive methods and tools for monitoring speech pathologies
  • Benchmarks and datasets for evaluating voice pathology detection systems
  • Data collection and annotation techniques for speech pathology research
  • Adaptation of foundation models for speech pathology detection and analysis
