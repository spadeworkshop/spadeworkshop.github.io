Speech Pathology Analysis and DEtection (SPADE)

Hyderabad, India

Co-located with ICASSP 2025

Welcome to SPADE 2025, a workshop dedicated to the analysis and detection of speech pathologies, co-located with the International Conference on Acoustics, Speech, and Signal Processing (ICASSP 2025) in Hyderabad, India.

This workshop focuses on the latest advancements in analyzing speech-related pathologies, emphasizing practical applications that improve the lives of individuals with specific conditions. Topics include detecting voice pathologies, identifying neurodegenerative diseases, and classifying the severity of these conditions by analyzing speech recordings. The workshop explores how machine learning and deep learning techniques can be applied to these tasks, highlighting methods that significantly impact diagnosis, management, and support for individuals with speech-related pathologies.
